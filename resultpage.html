Resulting Page
Now that your teams have been created, you must now load up your selected version of FIFA and manually transfer the players to each certain team. For example, here are the steps for performing this in FIFA 19.

Step 0: Load up FIFA 19 to the Home Page

Step 1: Navigate to the Customize tab

Step 2: Select the Edit Teams section

Step 3: Select the Club Transfer section

Step 4: Select your desired team

Step 5: Select a player that should not be on your team but is currently there, and put them on a different team

Note: If your team reaches the minimum number of players allowed by the game, then skip to Step

Step 6: Repeat Step 5 until all players that should not be on your team are no longer there

Step 7: Search for a player that is on your Draft team, select that player, and add them to your desired team

Note: If your desired team has too many players, go back to Step 5

Step 8: Repeat Step 7 until all players on your Draft team are on nyour desired team

Note: At this point, your desired team should match your Draft team exactly

Step 9: Repeat Steps 4-8 until all teams that participated in the Draft have been edited to match each desired team

Step 10: Enjoy playing with your new Draft teams in tournament modes, Head-to-Head matches, or however else you would like!
